About

My profile brings together software engineering practice and applied research in data-intensive systems. I started by building microservice-based architectures and working on data integration problems, where I learned to design and deliver reliable systems in real-world environments. This evolved into applying semantic technologies and AI methods to extract meaningful information from complex data, including work on ontology alignment, knowledge graph modeling, and data quality in graph-based and NoSQL systems. Alongside this, I worked as a full-stack developer and Scrum Master in industry, contributing to production systems for public administration and gaining experience in delivering robust software within agile teams. Today, I combine these experiences to design and build intelligent data systems, integrating knowledge graphs, validation techniques (SHACL/SPARQL), and LLM approaches such as RAG and MCP to create solutions that are both theoretically grounded and practically useful.
